Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is an iconic monument located in Mumbai, overlooking the Arabian Sea. Built in the early 20th century, it is a symbol of Mumbai's rich history and serves as a major tourist attraction. The Gateway of India is a popular tourist attraction in Mumbai. It was built in the early 20th century and is located overlooking the Arabian Sea. The monument holds historical significance and is a symbol of Mumbai's rich history. Visitors can enjoy the stunning views and learn about the city's past. Don't miss the opportunity to explore this iconic landmark during your visit to Mumbai.

  • Location: Apollo Bandar, Colaba, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400001
  • Hours: Open 24 hours
  • Admission: Free

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a picturesque promenade in Mumbai, known for its stunning views of the Arabian Sea. It's a popular spot among locals and tourists alike, especially during sunset.

  • Location: Marine Drive, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400020
  • Hours: Open 24 hours
  • Admission: Free
