Events in Mumbai

Mumbai hosts a diverse range of events throughout the year, catering to various interests and passions. From cultural festivals and music concerts to art exhibitions and sports tournaments, there's always something happening in the city that never sleeps.
One of the highlights of Mumbai's event calendar is the Ganesh Chaturthi festival, celebrated with great pomp and fervor. The city comes alive with colorful processions, elaborate pandals, and the iconic immersion of Ganesha idols in the sea.
Music enthusiasts flock to Mumbai for the NH7 Weekender, one of India's largest music festivals, featuring a lineup of local and international artists across various genres. The festival promises a weekend of non-stop music, food, and fun.
Art lovers can explore Mumbai's vibrant cultural scene at events like the Kala Ghoda Arts Festival, a multi-disciplinary arts festival showcasing visual arts, music, dance, theater, and literature. Held annually in February, it attracts artists and enthusiasts from around the world.
Sports fans have plenty to cheer about with events like the IPL (Indian Premier League) matches held at the Wankhede Stadium. Cricket fever grips the city as top teams battle it out for glory in this high-octane cricket tournament.

Festivals in Mumbai

  • Ganesh Chaturthi: One of the most significant festivals in Mumbai, Ganesh Chaturthi celebrates the birth of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. It is marked by colorful processions, elaborate pandals (temporary structures), and the immersion of Ganesha idols in water bodies.
  • Navratri: Navratri is a nine-night festival dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga and her various forms. In Mumbai, Navratri is celebrated with vibrant garba and dandiya raas dances, traditional music performances, and elaborate decorations.
  • Holi: Holi, the festival of colors, is celebrated with much fervor and enthusiasm in Mumbai. People come together to splash colored powders and water on each other, dance to festive music, and indulge in traditional sweets and snacks.
