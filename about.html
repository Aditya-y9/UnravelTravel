About Us

We are a group of students from the University of Mumbai who have come together to create a website that will help tourists and locals alike to explore the city of Mumbai. Our website is a one-stop destination for all the information you need to know about Mumbai. From the best places to visit to the best places to eat, we have it all. Our website is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate. We hope you enjoy using our website and that it helps you make the most of your time in Mumbai.
